About Signal Processing in Finance

What is Fourier Transform?

The Fourier Transform is a mathematical technique that decomposes a signal into its constituent frequencies. In finance, it helps identify cyclical patterns in price movements.

Understanding Power Spectra

The power spectrum shows the strength of different frequency components in the data. Peaks in the spectrum indicate dominant cycles in the stock price.

Applications in Trading

Identifying market cycles can help with market timing, trading strategies, and understanding long-term market behavior.
